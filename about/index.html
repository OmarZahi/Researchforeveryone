Mission

Research for Everyone exists to make hands‑on science accessible. We provide a clear pathway for students and early‑career researchers to get real laboratory experience through standardized safety training and supervised sessions at partner labs.

Approach

We align with each host lab’s SOPs, enforce strict supervision ratios and access controls, and keep all activities within BSL‑1 and basic digital fabrication tools.
