Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This website collects only the information necessary to provide our services, such as names, email addresses and consent status. We do not sell your data and only share it with partner labs to facilitate bookings and ensure safety compliance. For minors, guardian consent information is stored as required by law. We may collect usage statistics to improve our services, but these are anonymized and do not identify individual users. If you have questions or would like us to delete your data, please contact us.
